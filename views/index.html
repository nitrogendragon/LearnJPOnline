What is LearnJPOnline?

LearnJPOnline is a passion project to help myself study japanese more effectively. While initially it will be basic much like this description, I plan to ramp it up progressively adding more categories and features as I develop it. I hope that this site will be able to help myself and anyone else who finds it to gain the knowledge they desire and make potential trips to Japan a more pleasant and enjoyable experience.
